Final Project - Paper Prototypes

For my form interaction, the user will fill in their information and choose an amount to donate. When they press the donate button, a thank-you pop-up box will appear. For my calculation interaction, the user will enter a number to calculate their environmental impact, then the information will display underneath. For my homepage interaction, the user can scroll or click on the right side navigation to go to the next section. There's a section that shows different tips on how to be more sustainable. The user will have to click on a circle for a popup box to show more detailled facts.
