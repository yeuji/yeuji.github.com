Plastic is bad

90% of plastic polluting our oceans is carried by just 10 rivers. Green sea turtles now ingest twice the plastic they did 25 years ago. According to the United Nations, ingestion of plastic kills an estimated 1 million marine birds and 100,000 marine animals each year. Additionally, more than 90% of all birds and fish are believed to have plastic particles in their stomach. Plastic breaks up into tiny pieces in the sea, which are then consumed by fish and other sea animals.

Fact 1: A person eats 70,000 microplastics each year.

Fact 2: 73% of beach litter worldwide is plastic.

Fact 3: 2 million plastic bags are used every minute.

Fact 4: Plastic kills 1.1 million seabirds and animals.
